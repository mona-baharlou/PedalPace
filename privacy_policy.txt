Privacy Policy for PedalPace
Last Updated: December 22, 2025

PedalPace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
Precise Location Data: To provide accurate local weather forecasts and calculate your cycling suitability score, PedalPace requires access to your device's location (GPS). This data is used only while the app is in use and is not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Information
Weather Forecasting: Your location is sent to the OpenWeather API to fetch real-time meteorological data.

AI Insights: Weather parameters (temperature, wind, etc.) are processed via the Google Gemini API to generate cycling "Pro-Tips." No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to the AI engine.

Score Calculation: Data is processed locally on your device to determine your cycling suitability score.

3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party providers:

OpenWeatherMap: For weather data 

Google Gemini / Google Play Services: For AI insights and location accuracy (Privacy Policy)

4. Data Retention
PedalPace does not create user accounts or store your location history on any external database. Your data remains on your device and is cleared when the app is closed or uninstalled.

5. Permissions
You can grant or revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. Please note that if permissions are denied, the app will be unable to fetch weather data.
